📘 HOW TO CREATE A DISCORD BOT AND GET THE TOKEN

Follow these steps to create your bot and get its token:

1. 🔧 Open Discord Developer Portal:
   - Visit: https://discord.com/developers/applications
   - Log in with your Discord account.
   - Click on “New Application”.
   - Give your bot a name and click “Create”.

2. 🤖 Create the Bot:
   - In the sidebar, click “Bot”.
   - Click “Add Bot” > “Yes, do it!”

3. 🔑 Get Your Bot Token:
   - In the "Bot" tab, click “Reset Token” or “Copy Token”.
   - Copy the token and paste it in your config.json file like this:
     "bot_token": "PASTE_YOUR_TOKEN_HERE"

   ⚠️ IMPORTANT: Never share your bot token with anyone!

4. ⚙️ Enable Privileged Intents (Required for most bots):
   - In the same “Bot” tab, scroll down and enable:
     - SERVER MEMBERS INTENT
     - MESSAGE CONTENT INTENT

5. 🔗 Invite Bot to Your Server:
   - Go to the “OAuth2” tab > “URL Generator”.
   - Under scopes, check:
     ✅ bot
     ✅ applications.commands
   - Under permissions, choose what your bot needs (e.g., Read Messages, Send Messages, Manage Channels).
   - Copy the generated URL and open it in your browser.
   - Select your server and click “Authorize”.

6. ✅ Done! Your bot is now created and ready to be run using Python.

TIP: To run the bot, make sure you installed required libraries and filled in config.json properly.
